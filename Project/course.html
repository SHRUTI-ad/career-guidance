Eligibility Criteria

MBA

2 years

1. Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university.

2. Minimum aggregate of 50% is required for general category candidates and 45% for other reserved categories.

3. Graduation final year candidates are also eligible for an MBA.

4. Professional Degree holders such as CA/CS/CMA/ICWAI and others can also pursue an MBA.

MTech

2 years

1. BE/BTech degree from a recognized university. 2. Master’s degree in applied science is also eligible for an MTech Programme.

PGDM

2 years

1. Undergraduate course in any discipline from a recognized institution are eligible for PGDM.

2. Candidates must have secured a minimum aggregate of 50% in their bachelor’s degree.

3. Considerable knowledge of mathematics, English, and logical capability for entrance exam.

Machine Learning

6-18 months

1. Candidates who completed bachelor’s in Artificial Intelligence or any equivalent discipline from a recognized institution.

2. Entrance exams like GATE, SUAT, IIT-JEE, AND UPSEE have to be cleared.

MCA / MSc Computer Science

6-18 months

1. Candidates with graduation in BSc Honours or BCA or any equivalent discipline from a recognized institution.

2. BSc general students

3. Merit and entrance exam-based admission

2. Entrance exams like GATE, SUAT, IIT-JEE, AND UPSEE have to be cleared.
